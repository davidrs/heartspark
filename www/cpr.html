Emergency Response

1. Check victim for responsiveness

  1. unresponive
  2. not breathing
  3. not breathing normally

2. Call 911

3.A Perform manual CPR

  • Pump: If the victim is still not breathing normally, coughing or moving, begin chest compressions. Push down in the center of the chest 2 inches 30 times. Pump hard and fast at the rate of at least 100/minute, faster than once per second.
  • Blow: Tilt the head back and lift the chin. Pinch nose and cover the mouth with yours and blow until you see the chest rise. Give 2 breaths. Each breath should take 1 second.
  • CONTINUE WITH 30 PUMPS AND 2 BREATHS UNTIL HELP ARRIVES

3B. Find an AED

  • Most police cars have AEDs.
  • Use this app's map or list view to find the nearest.
  • Look for symbols with a heart and lightning.
